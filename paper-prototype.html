First Iteration

The feedback I received on my initial prototype was fairly straightfoward, partially because I provided a slightly too simple task. They said I should swap the order of the search bar and the recipes/restaurants toggle. I also gathered that there should be some form of feedback on clicking a link that's already active, like the "recipe" tab. I also had stupidly forgotten to include an image of the recipe on the recipe page.

Second Iteration

The second iteration had some better feedback. They thought the recipe icons read as categories due to the "culture" labels that I had added. Categories here isn't a bad idea, but it'll need some modification. It would also be a good thing to include a way to filter by a variety of categories. Genre, time of day, kind of food, and geographic areas were some of the suggestions. They also advised adding a header to the whole page; what the website does is unclear without one. They also thought a map for geopgraphic filtering would be an interesting feature.
